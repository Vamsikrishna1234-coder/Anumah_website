Our Story

Founded in 2010, BuildCraft Construction began as a small residential contractor with a vision to transform the construction landscape. Over the past decade, we've grown into a full-service construction company, handling everything from luxury homes to major commercial developments.With more than 15+ years of dedicated service, we have built a reputation for excellence by combining traditional craftsmanship with the latest technological advancements to create sustainable, efficient, and long-lasting structures. Our team of architects, engineers, and skilled professionals work tirelessly to bring client visions to life while maintaining the highest levels of safety, precision, and environmental responsibility.

Meet Our Founder

Mr. Gowd, the visionary founder of Anumah Infra, has been shaping skylines for over 15+ years. He combines innovation with quality to deliver landmark residential and commercial projects. His leadership philosophy focuses on transparency, customer satisfaction, and sustainable development. Under his guidance, Anumah Infra has successfully completed hundreds of projects across the region. He believes in creating communities, not just buildings, fostering trust with every client. Mr. Gowd emphasizes modern design, cutting-edge technology, and attention to detail in every project. His commitment to excellence has earned numerous awards and industry recognition. He actively mentors his team, nurturing talent and fostering a culture of creativity. Beyond real estate, he engages in social initiatives that contribute to community growth. Driven by passion and vision, Mr. Gowd continues to redefine urban living experiences.
